Recipe for Homemade Chicken Schnitzel

Description

Get flour, eggs and Panko breadcrumps. Put each in its own bowl, then take out the chicken schnitzel (2cm thick maximum) and season with pepper,salt and sweet paprika
After, put the chicken in the flour first, then the egg (make sure everything is covered with egg), and then into the breadcrumps
Put the chicken into the hot oil (the oil shouldn't be too hot, you dont want to burn the chicken, also dont use too little oil), and turn once the edges get golden brown

After you take out the chicken, put them on a piece of paper that can soak up any oil. Now is also the time to prepare the fried potatoes. For that, peel potatoes
and cut them in small pieces, wether those are discs or just triangles is up to you, the pieces should not be bigger than 3cm for easier and faster frying
Fry the potatoes in a bit of oil, season with salt, pepper, sweet paprika, garlic powder, and a little thymen. Add thymen again at the end.

    Ingredients

  • Chicken schnitzel (or breasts cut thin)
  • Flour
  • 2 Eggs
  • Panco breadcrumps
  • salt, pepper, sweet paprika powder and thymen
  • potatoes
  • frying oil

    Steps

  1. cut chicken
  2. dip chicken into flour first, then egg, then breadcrumps
  3. fry the chicken in oil
  4. take out chicken and let unnecessary oil drip onto paper
  5. cut, fry and season potatoes
  6. serve potatoes with the schnitzels

Bonustip: Serve with Chipotle Chili Sauce
